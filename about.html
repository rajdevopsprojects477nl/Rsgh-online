About RSGH

Empowering the next generation of Software Test Engineers

Our Mission

Ready Start Go Head (RSGH) was founded in 2025 with a simple goal: to provide high-quality, free resources for anyone looking to break into the tech industry, specifically in Software Testing and Quality Assurance.

We believe that career guidance and technical education should be accessible to everyone. Our platform offers tutorials, career advice, and AI-powered tools to help you succeed in your professional journey.

What We Offer

📚 Tutorials

Comprehensive guides on manual and automation testing, tools like Selenium and Playwright, and industry best practices.

🤖 AI Tools

Cutting-edge AI assistants like NLGuide AI for expats and AI Resume Tailor for job seekers.
