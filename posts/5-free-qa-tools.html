5 Free Tools Every QA Engineer Should Know

Published: March 10, 2026 • 4 min read

Efficiency is key in software testing. Having the right tools in your arsenal can save you hours of work. Here are five free tools that every modern QA engineer should be using in 2026.

1. Selenium IDE / Playwright

For quick automation and recording, these browser extensions are invaluable. They allow you to record your actions and generate scripts in various languages.

2. Postman

Postman remains the industry standard for API testing. The free version is extremely powerful, allowing you to create collections, run tests, and automate your API workflows.

3. Lighthouse

Performance and accessibility testing are more important than ever. Lighthouse, built into Chrome DevTools, provides a comprehensive report on your web page's performance.

4. Axe DevTools

Another essential for accessibility. Axe helps you identify accessibility issues early in the development cycle, ensuring your product is usable for everyone.

5. Trello / Jira (Free Tier)

Managing your tasks and bugs is crucial. These project management tools help you stay organized and keep track of your testing progress.
